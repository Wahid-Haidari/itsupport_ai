    Canvas Support

    What does Canvas support?

    Not all Canvas-related issues are handled by OU IT. For some issues, you will need to contact Canvas directly for support. Please contact Canvas if you need assistance with the following:

    • Adding/changing quizzes
    • Copying course content.
    • Copying course content from an old course.
    • Grade issues
    • Discussion boards usage
    • Creating news items
    • Dropbox usage (within Canvas)
    • Managing files and folders in their course
    • Questions regarding non-standard courses.

    How do I contact Canvas?

    • Canvas Support Hotline: 1-844-629-6842
    • The Help option on the Global Navigation bar in Canvas (where Account, Dashboard, Courses, etc. are located)
