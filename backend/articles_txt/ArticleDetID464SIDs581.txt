How to Access Account Management Through ONE
Tags
ONE
In order for a student to access Account Management through ONE, they will need to log in to Single Sign-On at
one.ou.edu
:
On the home page, click the student’s picture in the upper right corner, and click “Account Settings”.
Manage desired account settings on the Account Management page.
