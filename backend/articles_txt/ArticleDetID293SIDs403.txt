TeachAnywhere – Online Teaching
The University of Oklahoma has launched a new portal to help provide direction and answers with the online learning initiative. Information on TeachAnywhere can be found at
http://www.ou.edu/teachanywhere
. A full breakdown of relevant sites, applications and support information can be found at the site above. It also includes best practices and helpful guides to provide direction for online teaching.
The two main tools for online learning are
Canvas
, OU’s learning management system, and
Zoom
, a video conferencing tool. Test that you are able to
login
and
download Zoom
to your own device.
Access to Canvas is available at
https://canvas.ou.edu
. Zoom is available as a
desktop application
for PC and Mac and mobile application on
iOS
and
Android
.
