Logging into Zoom From Your Browser or Client
Logging into Zoom From Your Browser or Client
Login from Browser.
Navigate to
https://oklahoma.zoom.us/
Click Sign In.
Type in OU 4×4 and Password.
Login from Client:
Open Zoom client.
Click Sign In, then Sign In with SSO.
Type in Oklahoma
User will then have web browser open.
Type 4×4 and Password.
User will be prompted to Open zoom.us, click Open Zoom.us
Client will show as having been logged in.
After having successfully logged in, access Zoom link shared by professor and join course.
For information about creating a free Zoom account, please visit
