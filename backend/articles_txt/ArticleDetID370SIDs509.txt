Kaltura Capture: Overview
Kaltura Capture: Overview
Kaltura Capture combines painless recording, automated publishing, and interactive viewing to enable easy video capture in class, at the office, at home, or on-the-go. It is an end-to-end application with seamless recording-to-viewing, automatically captures audio/screen/PowerPoint and video, and allows interactive viewing through side-by-side picture-in-picture viewing.
Content creators capture multi-stream recordings of up to three video inputs synchronized with a PowerPoint presentation or screen capture.
Viewers can navigate to desired content, search through slide text, switch between video streams, and view picture in picture.
CaptureSpace
makes it simple to create searchable, interactive video that can be viewed on any device at any time.
FEATURES:
Pick and choose from a set of video widgets or components: media upload widget, clipping, trimming and thumbnail editing tools, a video gallery, video embed tool for WYSIWYG editors, video search, video analytics and more.
All components are built with full cross-device and mobile support, responsive design and automatic device detection
All components can be easily skinned and designed, as well as localized for international use
Easily connects with plugins, developed both by Kaltura and Kaltura partners.
Hosted on the Kaltura cloud – all new features and developments are automatically updated for all applications using the framework
