Register a Wired Device
Register a Wired Device
Tags
NetReg
The following instructions will allow you to register any network-enabled device with a browser, such as a desktop or laptop computer to the OU
wired
network.
If you wish to register a computer or device on the OU
wireless
network, please visit the
Wifi@OU
instructions.
To manually register a network device which may not have a browser, such as a video game console, please visit visit
https://net-reg.ou.edu
(opens a new window) from a device
with a browser
. Once logged in, you will be able to manually register these additional devices. You will need to find these additional devices’ MAC addresses when registering. Instructions on locating the MAC address for your device can be
found here
(opens a new window).
You must have an active OUNet Account to complete the NetReg process.
Register a Device with a Browser
The following article will provide step-by-step instructions on how to add your device at NetReg such as desktop, laptop, PlayStation or Xbox.
Step 1
: Navigate to
https://net-reg.ou.edu
Please enter your ‘
OUNetID’
in the Username field.
Provide your ‘
Password’
(Enter your OUNetID password)
Next, click-on
Log On
button to continue.
Step 2
: If you are logging in for the first time, please read and select
‘I agree with the terms and conditions’
and click on
Submit
to continue.
Step 3
: Click on ‘
Register a Device
’ tab to register a new device in NetReg.
Step 4
: Please, fill in the required details in the below tabs to add a device
Note
: if you don’t know the MAC address for your device. Please, click on ‘How do I find my Mac address’ option or click the below link to view the instructions:
https://itsupport.ou.edu/TDClient/30/Unified/KB/ArticleDet?ID=134
Select a device category from the above drop-down list.
Select a device type from above list
Above list appears only when you have selected a particular device category.
Please, click on ‘Add’ button to register your device in NetReg.
Step 5
: After successful completion, you will be redirected to the below screen.
This message shows you that the device is successfully registered in NetReg.
If you have multiple devices, you can sort them by date, description, and MAC address.
You can also register a new device by clicking on ‘
Register a Device’
tab.
Here, you can verify the details of your latest device registration like MAC address, Description, and Device Type.
Register a Device without a Browser
To manually register a network device without a browser, such as a PlayStation or Xbox, visit
https://net-reg.ou.edu
(opens a new window) from a device
with a browser (such as a laptop, smartphone or tablet).
Once logged in, you will be able to manually register these additional devices.
You will need to find these additional devices’ MAC addresses when registering. Instructions on locating the MAC address for your device can be
found here
(opens a new window).
