Autodesk Software Downloads
The latest editions of Autodesk software are available directly from the Autodesk website at
http://www.autodesk.com/education/home
. Simply create an education account and you’ll be able to select from 3 different software suites.
From the main page click Free Software.
Next select the software you wish to download and select to “Get It Now”.
Now create an account and follow the on screen prompts to download the installer for your operating system.
