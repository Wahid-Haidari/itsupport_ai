WorkAnywhere – Working Remotely
The University of Oklahoma has launched a new portal to help provide direction and answers for those employees asked to work remotely. Information on WorkAnywhere can be found at
http://www.ou.edu/workanywhere
. A full breakdown of relevant sites, applications and support information can be found at the site above. It also includes best practices for security and accessibility.
