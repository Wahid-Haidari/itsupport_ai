How do I access my mail through one.ou.edu?
How do I access my mail through one.ou.edu?
Tags
ONE
OU Mail through Office 365 is available by going to
https://outlook.office365.com
.
If you are currently on the One website and wish to navigate to your OU e-mail through that site, move your cursor to the upper right hand corner and click the Application & Tools square icon.
This will open a navigation menu with various tools, select OU email to open your Office 365 account.
