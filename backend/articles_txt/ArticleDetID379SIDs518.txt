Add captions to a video on MyMedia
Add captions to a video on MyMedia
How to add captions to a video on MyMedia:
Log in at mymedia.ou.edu with your
OUNetID and password
Click on your name to access a drop-down menu
Select My Media from the menu
Click
Edit
on the video requiring captions
Select the
Captions
tab
Click
Upload captions file
Click
Browse
Select the proper caption file
Use the drop-down to select the proper language
Provide a label for the caption selector (typically the language; ex: English)
Click
Save
More information and resources
:
You can edit the existing caption for all videos in MyMedia if the caption exists already.
Read our article on
updating/editing MyMedia video captions
.
You can also
request
ADA compliant captions.
Learn more about
requesting ADA compliant captions
.
