
  Add captions to a video on MyMedia


How to add captions to a video on MyMedia:






Log in at mymedia.ou.edu with your OUNetID and password 


Click on your name to access a drop-down menu 


Select My Media from the menu


Click Edit on the video requiring captions 








Select the Captions tab 




 




Click Upload captions file 




 




Click Browse 








Select the proper caption file  


Use the drop-down to select the proper language 


Provide a label for the caption selector (typically the language; ex: English) 


Click Save 




 




More information and resources:  




You can edit the existing caption for all videos in MyMedia if the caption exists already. Read our article on updating/editing MyMedia video captions (https://itsupport.ou.edu/TDClient/30/Unified/KB/ArticleDet?ID=302). 


You can also request ADA compliant captions. Learn more about requesting ADA compliant captions (https://itsupport.ou.edu/TDClient/30/Unified/KB/ArticleDet?ID=2514). 


Machine Captions can be ordered for videos in MyMedia. Get the details on ordering Machine Captions (https://itsupport.ou.edu/TDClient/30/Unified/KB/ArticleDet?ID=1111).
