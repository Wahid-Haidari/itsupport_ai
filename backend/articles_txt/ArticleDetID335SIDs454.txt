Create.ou.edu Support
OU Create
, a project managed by the University of Oklahoma’s Office of Digital Learning, offers students, faculty, and staff the opportunity to build and maintain a digital identity by facilitating both the registration of web domains (.com, .org, .net) or subdomain and easy installation of popular open-source applications such as WordPress, MediaWiki, Drupal, and Omeka.
If you need support with create.ou.edu, please contact OU Create through the following
form
or e-mail
create@ou.edu
.
