Learning Spaces Technology Support
provides specialized in-room support and training services to assist the University of Oklahoma community with the technical aspects of technology use in classrooms, meeting rooms and collaborative settings across campus.
Learning Spaces support priority is based upon the following hierarchy.
Centrally Scheduled Classrooms
Non – Managed Classrooms / Partnership Agreement Engagement Spaces
All other meeting rooms & collaborative spaces
For issues while class is in session or if you need immediate assistance with support in an OU IT learning space, please call us at (405) 325-HELP (4357).
