Adobe Mobile Applications
OU’s Adobe enterprise account now offer Adobe Cloud Storage, which enables the use of iOS and Android apps to save images to the Adobe Cloud. Apps like Photoshop express will also have a Creative Cloud button that will allow you to sign in with your OU email and password.
