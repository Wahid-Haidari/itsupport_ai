
  Adobe Package Request


At present only full time OU employees may use Adobe enterprise. There still may be a way for you to get Adobe installed on your machine however. If you use an OU owned machine or are a department running a computer lab and need Adobe products installed, you can request an Adobe package installation by visiting the Adobe page on the IT Software Catalog.

https://itsupport.ou.edu/TDClient/30/Unified/Requests/ServiceDet?ID=46 (https://itsupport.ou.edu/TDClient/30/Unified/Requests/ServiceDet?ID=46)

Click the above link, then click the Support tab, then the Request Custom Package button. Fill out the form with the requested information and our specialists will be touch to talk about your request.
