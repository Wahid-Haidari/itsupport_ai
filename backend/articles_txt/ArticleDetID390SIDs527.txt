Upload content to MyMedia
Upload content to MyMedia
Click on + Add New (left side to the account icon) > Media Upload.
Click the
Choose a file to upload
button
Select the file to be added to MyMedia
Complete the following:
Name:
title for the video
Description:
description of the media
Tags:
common keywords or terms associated with the media
Access:
who should be able to access the media
Click
Save
You can then:
Click
Go To Media
to find the latest upload
Click
Choose another file
to upload another media item.
Note: Larger files can sometimes take 2-3 attempts before they finish uploading. If you are having issues,
submit the request
to services.
