Canvas – Course Navigation – About Course Navigation
Canvas – Course Navigation – About Course Navigation
Students
: If you are enrolled in a course as a student, the Course Home Page helps you navigate your course and manage your coursework.
Instructors
: As an instructor, the Course Home Page is the first page students see when they open the course. The Home Page also helps students understand how they can navigate through the course. You can customize the Home Page to create a specific workflow for your students.
