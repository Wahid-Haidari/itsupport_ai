Enabling Long Passwords on iOS
Enabling Long Passwords on iOS
To enable long passwords (any password longer than a 4 digit “simple passcode”), you will need to do the following:
If you do not have a current passcode on your device:
Go to
Settings
>
General
>
Passcode Lock
Slide the “
Simple Passcode
” option to “Off”
Tap “
Turn Passcode On
” and enter your desired password
You will be prompted for this password again for confirmation
If you do have a current passcode on your device:
Go to
Settings
>
General
>
Passcode Lock
You will be prompted for your current passcode
Slide the “
Simple Passcode
” option to “Off”
You will be prompted for your current passcode for confirmation
Enter your desired password
You will be prompted for this password again for confirmation
