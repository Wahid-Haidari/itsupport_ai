University Owned Computer Repairs
OU IT is pleased to provide warranty hardware service work on all University-owned Apple desktops and laptops, along with all Dell desktop and laptops. We can also attempt to provide hardware service work on out-of-warranty systems. Please see
this page
more additional information.
If you are in need of support for your university owned device, please feel free to submit a request through our IT Service Catalog
here
so that we can set up an appointment at one of our Service Centers. Alternately you may call us at 325-HELP(4357) to begin this process or if you have any additional questions.
Click Here
for our Service Centers hours and locations.
