
  University Owned Computer Repairs


OU IT is pleased to provide warranty hardware service work on all University-owned Apple desktops and laptops, along with all Dell desktop and laptops. We can also attempt to provide hardware service work on out-of-warranty systems. Please see this page (https://itsupport.ou.edu/TDClient/30/Unified/KB/ArticleDet?ID=56) more additional information.

If you are in need of support for your university owned device, please feel free to submit a request through our IT Service Catalog here (https://itsupport.ou.edu/TDClient/30/Unified/Requests/ServiceDet?ID=35) so that we can set up an appointment at one of our Service Centers. Alternately you may call us at 325-HELP(4357) to begin this process or if you have any additional questions. Click Here (https://itsupport.ou.edu/TDClient/35/Norman/Requests/ServiceDet?ID=124&SIDs=147) for our Service Centers hours and locations.
