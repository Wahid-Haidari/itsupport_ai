Connecting to WIFI@OU with Linux
WIFI@OU Configuration on Fedora 27, ignoring CA certificate authentication. To connect to WIFI@OU on a Linux machine, please do the following:
Go to Settings -> Wi-Fi
Click the gear icon next to “WIFI@OU”
Enter the following:
Security: WPA & WPA2 Enterprise
Authentication: Protected EAP (PEAP)
CA Certificate: Check “No CA certificate is required”
PEAP Version: Automatic
Inner Authentication: MSCHAPv2
Username: (OUNet ID)
Password: (Password)
Click Apply, you should now connect.
