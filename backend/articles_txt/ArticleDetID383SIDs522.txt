Create a new screen capture in MyMedia
Create a new screen capture in MyMedia
Create a new screen capture recording in MyMedia.
Log in at mymedia.ou.edu with your OUNetID and password
At the top of the page, go to
Add New > Kaltura Capture
If you have not downloaded and installed Kaltura Capture Desktop Recorder, you may do so now
Once the Kaltura CaptureSpace Desktop Recorder has been installed, you will have to launch it from the website (Repeat
Add New > Kaltura Capture)
When Kaltura CaptureSpace Desktop Recorder has been launched, you can start recording by pressing the red round button.
Make sure to:
Select which screen (i.e. monitor) to capture, and whether it will be the full screen or a selected area
If it is a selected area, you will be prompted to choose the area
Select an audio source if applicable
Once you have made your selections, a countdown to the recording will appear, followed by a toolbar during the actual recording
​Click the
Stop
button (gray box) on the toolbar when you are finished. Then select
Yes, Stop it
You will be taken to a preview of your screen capture, where you can watch it
Here, you can also add a title, a description, and tags to the video
Next, you can
Save
,
Save & Upload
, or, if desired,
Delete
the capture.
After Saving and Uploading, you will be taken to the library, in which you can find previous recordings and links to them.
