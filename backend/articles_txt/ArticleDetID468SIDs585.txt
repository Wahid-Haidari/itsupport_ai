How do I access Financial Aid using one.ou.edu?
In order to access Financial Aid information through ONE, the student will need to log in to Single Sign-On at one.ou.edu.
Under the Financial tab, click “Financial Aid”.
Under Financial Aid, there are multiple options including “Apply for financial aid” and “Apply for scholarships”.
