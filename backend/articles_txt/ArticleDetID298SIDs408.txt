Adding MyMedia to Canvas Course Navigation
Adding MyMedia to Canvas Course Navigation
Navigate to your Canvas course and click on the
Settings
button located on the bottom of the left side menu.
Click on the
Navigation
tab located at the top of the page.
Scroll down until you see the
MyMedia
tool, click on the three dots, then select
Enable
.
You should now see the MyMedia tool in the top list on the page. You can drag and drop the menu items in the order of your preference.
