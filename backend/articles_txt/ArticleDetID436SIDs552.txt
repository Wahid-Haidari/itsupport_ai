
  Canvas – Profile and User Settings – About Profile and User Settings


Students and Instructors: Profile and User Settings let you control your personal information in Canvas. You can control this information in the Help Corner.

You can use your profile and user settings to:

View and edit your user settings, (https://guides.instructure.com/m/4212/l/41957?data-resolve-url=true&data-manual-id=4212) such as display name, language preference (https://guides.instructure.com/m/4212/l/91042?data-resolve-url=true&data-manual-id=4212), and time zone (https://guides.instructure.com/m/4212/l/218818?data-resolve-url=true&data-manual-id=4212)

Change your default email address (https://guides.instructure.com/m/4152/l/65392?data-resolve-url=true&data-manual-id=4152) or add an additional email address (https://guides.instructure.com/m/4152/l/41471?data-resolve-url=true&data-manual-id=4152) for notifications

Add a text (SMS) contact method (https://guides.instructure.com/m/4152/l/41472?data-resolve-url=true&data-manual-id=4152) for notifications

View or add a profile picture (https://guides.instructure.com/m/4212/l/41467?data-resolve-url=true&data-manual-id=4212)

Set Notifications (https://guides.instructure.com/m/4212/l/73162?data-resolve-url=true&data-manual-id=4212) for your favorite communication channels

Upload personal Files (https://guides.instructure.com/m/4212/l/41989?data-resolve-url=true&data-manual-id=4212)

Create and manage ePortfolios (https://guides.instructure.com/m/4212/l/42001?data-resolve-url=true&data-manual-id=4212) of your course work

Manage new user features (https://guides.instructure.com/m/4212/l/162488?data-resolve-url=true&data-manual-id=4212)

Download your assignment submissions (https://guides.instructure.com/m/4212/l/366521?data-resolve-url=true&data-manual-id=4212) from current and previous courses
