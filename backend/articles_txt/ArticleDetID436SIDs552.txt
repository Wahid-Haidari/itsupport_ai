Canvas – Profile and User Settings – About Profile and User Settings
Canvas – Profile and User Settings – About Profile and User Settings
Students and Instructors
: Profile and User Settings let you control your personal information in Canvas. You can control this information in the Help Corner.
You can use your profile and user settings to:
View and edit your
user settings,
such as display name,
language preference
, and
time zone
Change your
default email address
or
add an additional email address
for notifications
Add a
text (SMS) contact method
for notifications
View or add a
profile picture
Set
Notifications
for your favorite communication channels
Upload
personal Files
Create and manage
ePortfolios
of your course work
Manage new
user features
Download your
assignment submissions
from current and previous courses
