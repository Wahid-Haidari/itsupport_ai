Checking Zoom License Type
How to Check my Zoom License
Go to Oklahoma.zoom.us
Sign in with you 4×4 credentials
On your left-hand side of the screen, go to the
Profile
Scroll down, and look for the
User Type
description
If you would like to request Zoom Add-On licenses, such as Webinar and Large Meeting, these can be purchased for an additional cost on the
IT Service Catalog
.
