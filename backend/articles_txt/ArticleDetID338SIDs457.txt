Respondus Overview and Support
Respondus Overview and Support
Overview
Respondus
is a powerful tool for creating and managing exams that can be printed to paper or published directly to Blackboard, Canvas, and other learning systems. Exams can be created offline in a Windows environment, or moved from one LMS to another.
Respondus
offers several options for support for its products which include:
Demo videos, which include how-tos on creating questions, moving exams, and publishing to various learning systems, can be found here:
http://www.respondus.com/products/respondus/demos.shtml
Respondus Support is available here:
https://support.respondus.com/support/
A support ticket to Respondus can be submitted
here
.
Respondus Email Support is available through
support@respondus.com
.
Please note that while OU IT can provide
general support
regarding setting up Respondus in Canvas, questions beyond the initial technical setup of Respondus in a course should be handled by Respondus Support directly.
