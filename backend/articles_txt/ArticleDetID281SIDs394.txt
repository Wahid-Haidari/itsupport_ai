Adding a MyMedia video Link to Canvas
Adding a MyMedia video Link to Canvas
Navigate to mymedia.ou.edu
Log in by clicking “Guest” in the top-right corner, then “Login” from the dropdown menu. Then, click your name and then “My Media” from that dropdown menu.
Find the video you would like to make unlisted, and click on the title.
Click on the “Actions” arrow and click on “Publish”.
Click on “Unlisted” and hit “Save”.
Now copy the URL at the top of the screen and paste it into a Canvas page/module.
