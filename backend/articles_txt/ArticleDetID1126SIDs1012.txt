Media Gallery Moderation: Student Uploads
Once the student submits their media, a course moderator/instructor will receive an approval request. All pending approval submission will be listed in the Pending Tab.
C
heck the box on the left of the pending media
.
To approve or reject the submission
, c
lick on the approve or reject button respectively. (Note: approving the media automatically publishes it to the course media gallery and can be added to a course gallery playlist, if you choose to do so).
If a student visits the media gallery before moderators’ approval, student will receive a reminder that their media is still pending approval.
