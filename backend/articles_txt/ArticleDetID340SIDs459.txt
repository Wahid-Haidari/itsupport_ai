PeopleSoft Employee Self Service
PeopleSoft Employee Self Service
Employee Self-Service
allows OU employees to do the following:
View and print your earning statements and save them to your personal computer.
View and update address information.
View and update campus and phone numbers.
Updates made to the campus phone number in this system will also be reflected in the other campus databases. This includes the online faculty / staff directory at search.ou.edu and the global address book available in Outlook.
A campus phone number is required; cell and/or home phone numbers are not displayed to the public.
Enroll in Direct Deposit.
View paycheck information.
Create and update an emergency contact list.
View compensation data.
OU Norman program employees can update federal W-4 tax information.
OU Norman program employees can print duplicate W-2 wage and tax statements for years starting with 2006.
To access Employee Self-Service:
Log in at
http://selfservehc.ou.edu/
(opens new window).
You will need to authenticate with Ping Mulfi-Factor Authentication.
To learn more about PingID, see
this article
.
To learn more about what to expect when using Ping to access Employee Self-Service, see
this article
.
Only employees who are currently listed as active employees according this system will be able to log in.
Access should be available if the employee has reached their official start date and all hiring paperwork (including electronic approvals) is complete.
If you are unable to log in, contact the HR representative in your department to verify that all of your paperwork/approvals have been completed.
Access to the system begins after your start date and ends after your position is terminated.
Users who no longer work at OU that need to access W-2 tax information should
contact Payroll.
