
  Unresponsive links in ONE


If you are browsing the one.ou.edu site and experience unresponsive links when you attempt to navigate within the site, please pause or disable your ad blockers. Some ad blockers may affect one.ou.edu and prevent some links on the site from opening. To resolve these problems, set one.ou.edu as a “safe” site in your ad blocker, turn off your ad blocker or use an alternate browser without an ad blocker enabled.
