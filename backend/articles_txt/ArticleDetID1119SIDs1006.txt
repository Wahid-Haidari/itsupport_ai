Updating Media Gallery Video settings and features
To edit the video after being published
:
Hover over the course in the media gallery page
.
Click on the three dots at the bottom.
Then the pencil
icon
.
This
will allow
you
to
edit
the
video
settings.
Details
will allow
you to edit the
name and description
.
Publish
will allow you to choose where to publish the media
.
This feature allows you to publish your video in multiple courses.
Options
controls comments and clipping by choosing to click on the check mark
.
Collaboration is for adding a new co-editor, co-publisher, or co-viewer.
Captions is for adding new captions other than the automatically generated ones through MyMedia.
Replace Media
allows you to choose another video to replace the existing one, either by uploading from your personal computer
or
inserting
a
media URL.
Please note that replacing a media
will remove the original video and
OUIT
recommend
s
uploading a new one rather than repl
ac
ing.
