Creating Playlist in Media Gallery
Playlists are the collections of videos that are intended for users to watch in order or are related in some ways. It can also be used to organize contents for students. Playlists can be created only by the gallery's manager.
Note that only the course
moderator/instructor can create a playlist
in Media Gallery
