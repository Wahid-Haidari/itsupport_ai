LearnAnywhere – Online Learning
The University of Oklahoma has launched a new portal to help provide direction and answers with the online learning initiative. Information on LearnAnywhere can be found at
http://www.ou.edu/learnanywhere
. A full breakdown of relevant sites, applications and support information can be found at the site above.
The two main tools for online learning are
Canvas
, OU’s learning management system, and
Zoom
, a video conferencing tool. Test that you are able to
login
and
download Zoom
to your own device.
Access to Canvas is available at
https://canvas.ou.edu
. Canvas Student, the mobile applications, is available on
iOS
and
Android
.
Zoom is available as a
desktop application
for PC and Mac and mobile application on
iOS
and
Android
.
