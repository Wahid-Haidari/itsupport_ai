
  LearnAnywhere – Online Learning


The University of Oklahoma has launched a new portal to help provide direction and answers with the online learning initiative. Information on LearnAnywhere can be found at http://www.ou.edu/learnanywhere (http://www.ou.edu/learnanywhere). A full breakdown of relevant sites, applications and support information can be found at the site above.

The two main tools for online learning are Canvas (http://canvas.ou.edu/), OU’s learning management system, and Zoom (http://oklahoma.zoom.us/), a video conferencing tool. Test that you are able to login (https://itsupport.ou.edu/TDClient/30/Unified/KB/ArticleDet?ID=170) and download Zoom (https://support.zoom.us/hc/en-us/articles/201362233-Downloading-the-latest-Zoom-update) to your own device.

Access to Canvas is available at https://canvas.ou.edu (https://canvas.ou.edu/). Canvas Student, the mobile applications, is available on iOS (https://www.google.com/url?sa=t&rct=j&q=&esrc=s&source=web&cd=3&cad=rja&uact=8&ved=2ahUKEwj9kaD9npPoAhXQna0KHRGFCjoQFjACegQIBBAB&url=https%3A%2F%2Fapps.apple.com%2Fus%2Fapp%2Fcanvas-student%2Fid480883488&usg=AOvVaw0HZAHv8jvwxmyt7aBjFH70) and Android (https://www.google.com/url?sa=t&rct=j&q=&esrc=s&source=web&cd=4&cad=rja&uact=8&ved=2ahUKEwj9kaD9npPoAhXQna0KHRGFCjoQFjADegQIAhAB&url=https%3A%2F%2Fplay.google.com%2Fstore%2Fapps%2Fdetails%3Fid%3Dcom.instructure.candroid%26hl%3Den_US&usg=AOvVaw289b0odRSZdPmv0bTnZ8PB).

Zoom is available as a desktop application (https://oklahoma.zoom.us/download) for PC and Mac and mobile application on iOS (https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/id546505307) and Android (https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=us.zoom.videomeetings).
